## BLAgent: Agentic RAG for File-Level Bug Localization

At ESE Research Days 2026 held at the University of Calgary, I presented BLAgent as a Power Pitch showcasing our work on agentic retrieval-augmented generation for file-level bug localization.

BLAgent improves bug localization by combining repository structure-aware retrieval with agent-based reasoning over candidate code files. The goal is to better support downstream tasks such as bug triage and automated program repair in large-scale software systems.

Authors: Md Afif Al Mamun, Gias Uddin

Replication Package: [https://github.com/afifaniks/BLAgent](https://github.com/afifaniks/BLAgent)
