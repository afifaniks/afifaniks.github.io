<div class="stanza">
I think I love her the most when she's asleep<br>
Like a swan, like the tranquil of a night<br>
She breathes beside me, still as held light<br>
I lie awake and wonder —
</div>
<div class="stanza">
What have I ever done to deserve<br>
something as majestic as she
</div>
<p class="final-line">— Afif</p>
